Executive Summary

Over the reporting period, transaction volume averaged 125,000 API calls/day, with latency at 350 ms. Success held at 98.4 %, while failures rose to 1.6 %. Versus the prior period, volume grew 12 %, latency increased 5 %, and error rate climbed 0.4 %. We missed our 99 % uptime SLA by 0.6 %, though latency remains under 500 ms. Peak (11 am–1 pm AWST) saw queue backlogs and a 20 % surge in timeout tickets, impacting APAC mobile users.

Action Items

  • Provision an extra API cluster during 11 am–1 pm AWST to handle spikes.
  • Implement response-level caching on high-frequency endpoints to cut latency.
  • Add adaptive retry logic for transient failures and auto-flag persistent errors.
  • Configure real-time SLA alerts for success-rate dips below 99 %.
